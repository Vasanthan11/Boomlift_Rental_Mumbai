Boom lift categories

Articulated & Telescopic Boom Lifts for Your Mumbai Site.

Choose from articulated boom lifts for flexible up-and-over reach or telescopic boom lifts for straight, long-distance outreach. Our team will help match the right category based on obstacles, working height and ground conditions at your site in Mumbai.

Articulated Boom Lifts

Ideal when you need to reach up and over obstacles such as pipe racks, roofing, canopies or machinery inside factories and commercial buildings.

  • Up-and-over outreach around structures
  • Better maneuverability in tight spaces
  • Electric & diesel options for indoor / outdoor use

Typical working heights for articulated boom lifts range from approx. 40 ft to 135 ft, with platform capacities suited for tools and two operators.

Telescopic Boom Lifts

Best for open areas where you need maximum horizontal outreach and high vertical access on construction, refinery, port and infrastructure jobs in Mumbai.

  • Straight-line reach to high elevations
  • High stability for rough outdoor terrain
  • Ideal for bridges, chimneys, tanks & metros

Telescopic booms are commonly used where working heights can exceed 100 ft, enabling safe access for heavy-duty industrial and infrastructure projects.

Boom lift FAQs

Questions Mumbai Teams Commonly Ask.

A few quick answers that help when you’re planning boom lift rentals for your next project.

What information do you need to suggest a boom lift?

We typically need working height, approximate outreach, indoor or outdoor usage, ground conditions, access constraints and your expected rental duration. Site photos or sketches from your Mumbai project help us suggest the safest option.

Do you provide operators along with the boom lifts?

Yes, operator support can be included based on availability and job requirement. We can also coordinate with your safety team for basic orientation and controls familiarisation if your own certified operators will use the machine.

Can boom lifts be used at night or during shutdowns?

Many Mumbai projects run night shifts or tight shutdown windows. We’ll plan mobilisation, fueling and support to match your work schedule, subject to site access, permissions and safety norms.

How far in advance should I book a boom lift?

For standard heights, we can often support on short notice, but for high-reach or specialised booms it’s best to block machines in advance so availability and logistics are secured for your dates.
