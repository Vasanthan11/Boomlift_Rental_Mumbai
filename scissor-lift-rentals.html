Scissor Lift Rental FAQs for Mumbai

A few of the questions we regularly receive from project managers, safety teams and facility heads across Mumbai.

What information do you need to quote?

We typically ask for working height, indoor or outdoor application, estimated rental duration, floor conditions, and whether the work is during regular hours or night shifts. Site photos help us speed up the selection process.

Can scissor lifts be used on finished floors?

Yes, but we need to understand the floor finish and load capacity. Electric scissor lifts with non-marking tyres are commonly used in malls, showrooms and finished offices.

Do you provide operators with the machines?

Operator support can be added based on your requirement. Alternatively, your own trained operators can use the machine after basic familiarisation and adherence to safety guidelines.

How early should I book a scissor lift?

For common heights we can often support on short notice; for multiple machines or long-duration projects, blocking capacity in advance ensures guaranteed availability for your planned schedule in Mumbai.
